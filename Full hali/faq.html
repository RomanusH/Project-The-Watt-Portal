Frequently Asked Questions

What is cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency that uses cryptography for security and operates independently of a central bank.

How does blockchain work?

Blockchain is a distributed ledger technology that records transactions across multiple computers in a way that ensures the security, transparency, and integrity of the data.

What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is the first and most well-known cryptocurrency, created by an unknown person or group of people using the pseudonym Satoshi Nakamoto in 2009.
